Atropos is just for fun. Please don't take it too seriously. It's creator only wanted to inspire people to live each day as if it were their last. All statistics used were based off various census results.

Just type in your age and get a visual of how long you've lived and how long(psuedo-statistically) you have left to live.

**The name Atropos comes from the Grecian version of the Three Fates. She is the sister who cuts the thread.
